Read the Rules

  • In rock-paper-scissors, at the beginning each player receives three random cards.
  • The two players will each choose a card to play. There are three rounds in total.
    • The card with the fist represents rock.
    • The card with the hand flat represents paper.
    • The card with the hand holding out two fingers represents scissors.
  • The rules that determine which sign beats another are the following:
    • Rock wins over scissors
    • Scissors wins over paper
    • Paper wins over rock
